About us

We are a team of enthusiasts, geeks, thinkers and above all determined people aiming to work and try and bring about a change in the society. This is the official website for the team 'Q'rED,where you can check out our work and our team .

So, what exactly 'Q'rED is ?

'Q'rEDis an initiative that aims at reducing the extensive, never ending, blood boiling CNG refilling station queues . Yeah, that was serious exaggeration. But, we seriously aim at reducing those long queues at these station, by guiding our users to station that are less crowded through redirected ways and infact saving both money, time and resources.

How The App Works ?

Download the app

Search for QrED and Download the free App from Google Playstore or IOS App store.

Sign up

Create a free account and Login. your details will be stored on our secure servers and will not be shared to any third party.

Search

Activate your GPS and write the name of the area you want to search in the search tab.

That's it

you'll be provided with the real time traffic updates and the least congested route to find your ideal Petrol/CNG station.
It's that Simple.
